A showcase of my work and passion behind my animated series "Pyro, Alex, and Rob."

Watch on YouTube

In a vibrant world filled with zany adventures and unexpected challenges, “Pyro, Alex, and Rob” follows the comedic exploits of three unlikely friends: Pyro, a laid-back fire creature whose emotions ignite his flames; Alex, a clever human with a knack for resourcefulness and a “work smart, not hard” mentality; and Rob, a Scottish robot grappling with anxiety and OCD, whose extendable limbs add both utility and humor to their misadventures.

While the show features intense action and emotional depth, it often layers back to highlight the humor in their day-to-day lives.
